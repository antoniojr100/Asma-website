About

Are you experiencing symptoms of depression and /or anxiety, or other life challenges? I will guide you through your journey of recovery, helping you self-discover your unique path to healing. I specialize in trauma-informed and culturally sensitive approaches. I utilized Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Narrative Therapy, somatic intervention, and Interpersonal psychotherapy. أعمل أيضًا مع اللاجئين الذين يواجهون تحديات إعادة التوطين في كندا، و/أو لديهم مشاكل تتعلق بالصحة العقلية.

I will collaborate with you to establish trust, a good rapport, safety, and stability. I will actively listen to your perspective regarding your mental health concerns, understand, and validate your emotions. Please reach out to me.
