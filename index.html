About

Are you experiencing symptoms of depression and /or anxiety, or other life challenges? I will guide you through your journey of recovery, helping you self-discover your unique path to healing. I specialize in trauma-informed and culturally sensitive approaches. I utilize Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Narrative Therapy, somatic intervention, and Interpersonal psychotherapy.

Together, we will build a foundation of trust, a strong rapport, and a profound sense of safety and stability. Your voice will be heard, your perspective on your mental health concerns will be understood, and your emotions will be validated. Please take the first step and reach out to me.

With over five years of experience in clinical counselling, I have honed my skills in cognitive-behavioral therapy, trauma-informed model, solution-focused therapy, narrative therapy, and Interpersonal psychotherapy. You can trust that you are in capable hands, and I am here to guide you through your recovery journey.
